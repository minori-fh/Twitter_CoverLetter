My professional background has been mainly been focused on finance; I currently work at a top consulting firm focusing on identifying and mitigating financial risk.

I spent most of my days reviewing the code that engineers had written, identifying areas where there was risk, and documenting the process of how software applications stored and transferred financially relevant data.

Working with engineers on a daily basis was what finally gave me the courage and confidence that engineering is what I truly wanted to pursue. Fast forward six months later, and I am almost at the finish line to completing the Full-Stack Coding Bootcamp hosted by UC Berkeley.
